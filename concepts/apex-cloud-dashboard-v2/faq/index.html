Getting Started

Starting with ApexOneIQ

What is ApexOneIQ?

ApexOneIQ is an Executive Intelligence operating system that scans a business website, evaluates visibility and trust signals, prioritizes growth opportunities, and prepares the next actions an owner should review.

How do I start using ApexOneIQ?

Sign in with Google, enter your business website, and run the Executive Intelligence Scan. ApexOneIQ then opens the executive workspace with the score, findings, and recommended next action.

What happens after the scan?

ApexOneIQ turns scan evidence into an executive brief, mission queue, readiness status, and the next highest-confidence opportunity.

AI Visibility

Appearing in modern search and AI answers

What is AI visibility?

AI visibility measures how clearly a business can be understood, trusted, and cited by AI answer engines and modern search experiences.

How does ApexOneIQ improve AI visibility?

It evaluates structured data, content clarity, entity consistency, FAQ coverage, authority signals, and trust proof, then ranks the work that can improve answer-engine readiness.

Does ApexOneIQ fabricate rankings or traffic?

No. If rankings, competitors, traffic, revenue, or search volume cannot be verified, ApexOneIQ marks the data as pending rather than inventing it.

SEO

Search visibility, website health, and technical readiness

Does ApexOneIQ replace SEO tools?

ApexOneIQ is designed for executive action, not raw reporting. It uses scan evidence to prioritize what matters, explain why it matters, and prepare improvements through the mission workspace.

What SEO signals does ApexOneIQ scan?

ApexOneIQ can evaluate metadata, schema, headings, internal links, indexability, crawlability, sitemap, robots, trust signals, AI-readable content, and website health indicators.

How are recommendations prioritized?

Recommendations are ranked by measurable evidence, confidence, business impact, dependencies, risk, and whether the action can be prepared, approved, verified, or requires third-party confirmation.

Google Business Profile

Local trust and map visibility

Does ApexOneIQ help with Google Business Profile optimization?

Yes. ApexOneIQ identifies profile readiness issues, local trust gaps, citation opportunities, and business-information consistency items that can improve local visibility.

Can ApexOneIQ verify my Google Business Profile?

ApexOneIQ can prepare and prioritize the work, but third-party verification remains dependent on Google and may require owner action.

Security

Access, billing, and owner control

Is ApexOneIQ secure?

ApexOneIQ uses authenticated access for owner workspaces and keeps billing, OAuth, and subscription handling separate from public content.

Does ApexOneIQ store Stripe card data?

No. Customer payment information is collected by Stripe Checkout on Stripe-hosted payment pages, not by the ApexOneIQ server.

Pricing

Choosing the right plan

What does the free scan include?

The free scan gives a limited executive snapshot, including the Business Growth Score, website health context, AI visibility readiness, and upgrade prompts for deeper mission access.

Which plan should I choose?

DIY is for owners who want reports and implementation guidance. AI Automated is for businesses that want ApexOneIQ to prepare mission work. Concierge adds Apex team review and support.

Can Enterprise be purchased online?

No. Enterprise is request-information only and does not open Stripe Checkout from the self-service pricing flow.
